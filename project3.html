#3 - Experimental Clock

description:

I realized how the shadow changes from one to the other side as the time changes. If calculated the angle of the shadow could be a good timer, which works like the clock but it isn’t the clock. The best way to observe the change is to put a stick on the ground where the sunlight hits and see the change of the shadow rotating. I wanted to do the rotation in my work, so I searched up how people would make a clock in p5, then after I learned how to make the rotation I put in the sun and for the shadow I just had to put the line starting at the base of the object and make the x and y of the rotation the opposite of the sun, which can be done just by adding a “-”. I put the background change, so it shows the sun and when the background is at night the sun became the moon and there’s no shadows, because I think that at night people are sleeping so we don’t need to keep track of the time, so I made the background turn dark and the sun become the moon to show the different times.
